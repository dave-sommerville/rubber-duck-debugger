What's rubber duck debugging?

A technique, almost a ritual, of programmers is Rubber Duck Debugging. You see, when you're really engrossed in a project, especially if you wrote it, you might know it too well to truly SEE it any longer. Walking your duck familiar through the script, explaining your thoughts and code, you slow down and think about what is really happening. Another way to see it:
